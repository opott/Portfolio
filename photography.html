Photography

Photography is one of my favourite ways to express creativity and capture the beauty of the world around me. From landscapes to cityscapes, I enjoy exploring different perspectives and telling stories through my lens.

What I Like to Photograph

Architechture - I'm fascinated by the shapes, patterns, and symmetry of buildings. From historic structures to modern skyscrapers, I enjoy capturing the details and prespectives that make architechture so unique.

Nature - Exploring the outdoors is a big part of my photography. Whether it's landscapes, wildlife, or serene natural settings, I love finding ways to show the beauty of the natural world.

Railways - Railways are a perfect blend of engineering and storytelling. I enhoy photographing trains, stations, and tracks, capturing their movement, design, and the role they play in connecting people and places.

Why I Enjoy Photography

For me, photography is about more than just taking pictures - it's a way to slow down, notice the details, and share my perspective with others. Every photo tells a story!

My Equipment

I shoot with a Canon EOS 2000D, and use a combination of Picasa 3 and Affinity Photo 2 to organise and edit my photos.
