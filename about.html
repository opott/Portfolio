About Me

Hello! I'm Oliver, a passionate computer science and photography student who loves coding and capturing the beauty of the world through a camera. Based in the UK, I enjoy building cool projects and exploring my interests in technology, photography, and trains.

What I Do

Programming - I am constantly learning and building. Whether it's developing websites, contributing to open-source, or experimenting with hardware like the Raspberry Pi, I enjoy bringing ideas to life. I also run CourseNotes, an educational revision website.

Photography - When I'm not coding, you'll likely find me behind a camera, capturing stunning landscapes and architecture through my lens. Photography is my way of seeing and sharing the world's beauty.

Railway - I have a keen interest in trains and enjoy riding them as well as watching videos about their operations and history.

Cybersecurity - I'm deeply intrigued by the world of cybersecurity and aspire to pursue a career in this field. Protecting digital systems and solving security challenges fuels my passion for computing and digital innovation.

Passions

Hack Club - I'm a proud member of Hack Club, a global community of high school students passionate about coding and creativity. Hack Club empowers students to build projects, learn together, and create a culture of innovation through collaboration and community events. At my school, I run a Hack Club, where I encourage fellow students to explore coding, build exciting projects and share their work with others. From hands-on workshops to creative problem-solving, my goal is to inspire a love for technology in others.
